The Nino-OS Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Nino-OS, accessible via Discord, we prioritize the protection of our users' privacy. This privacy policy outlines the types of information collected and recorded when you use Nino-OS, and how it is used to enhance our services.

Log Records

Nino-OS utilizes a log record system where data such as command usage is collected. This includes the type of command used, Discord user & server IDs (NOT IP addresses), the channel (name and ID) in which the commands are used, Discord user & server names, user and server profile pictures, and timestamps of command usage. This information is collected to enhance our services and for protection purposes.

Database

Files such as command usage or system activities (such as the Giveaway, Counting, Auto Publish, Level, Ticket systems) are securely stored in an encrypted database to ensure protection.

If you need further information or assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us.
